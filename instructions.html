play sounds

do one of the hand signs with your left hand in the corresponding are to hear that sound

  • beat: one finger up like you're bouncing with the beat
  • beatbox: put your hand in front of your mouth like you are beatboxing
  • bubbles: put all you fingers together like a bubble that is going to pop
  • chime: show your fingers like you are playing the piano
  • comic: make your hand walk on the bottom of the screen like in a comic
  • drop: point your fingers down like you are dropping something
  • heartbeat: place your hand on you hart
  • knocking: show you fist like you are knocking on a door

sound boxes and actions

you can put certain sounds in a sound box play multiples sounds simultaneously, use your right hand for these commands

  • sound boxes
  • box[0]: make a 0 or a fist with your right hand
  • box[1]: put one index finger up
  • box[2]: index and middle finger up
  • box[3]: index, middle and ring finger or thumb
  • box[4]: index, middle, ring and pinky up
  • actions
  • pause: show your open palm
  • play: show you backhand

head gestures

with your head, you can control the volume, speed and looping, you need to hold the positions until you are satisfied with the settings (except for looping), when you don't want to do anything, make sure your head stays in the center

  • volume
  • higher: open your mouth as wide as possible
  • lower: frowning with your whole face and eyes closed
  • speed
  • higher: look to the right, or move your face all the way to the right side
  • lower: look to the left, or move your face all the way to theleft side
  • looping
  • on: tilt your head upwards
  • off: tilt your head downwards
